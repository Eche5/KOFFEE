Our Story

KOFFEE is an American company, founded by two brothers in
Modesto, California back in the 1950s. The organisation is still
very much a family one, but it has exploded to mammoth
proportions over the past few decades, and is the biggest
producer and distributor of California coffees in the world today.
Amazingly, we have over 75 subsidiary companies under its umbrella,
which range from the famous (eg Barefoot Coffee) to those which most
would swear to be tiny, independent coffee factories, such as Ghost
Vines and Turning Leaf.

Our vision is to continously be the leading coffee brands in
